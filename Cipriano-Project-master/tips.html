1. Keep a pouch of all important documents (Passport, Tickets, License,etc.)
2. Always pack a towel
3. Buy a small backback or suitcase with sturdy wheels
4. Take extra socks/under garments
5. Make sure all credit cards work and aren't expired prior to leaving
6. Use a map!
7. When you leave hotel, only take what you need
8. Lock your bags at all times
9. Bring extra copies of important documents
10. Learn basic phrases in the native language of your destination
11. Try local foods
12. Steer away from popular tourist sites/dining
13. Not direct flights are cheaper
14. Libraries, Starbucks, and other cafes have free wifi!
15. Always pack a basic first aid kit
16. Be open to strangers but keep your guard up
17. Try something new!
18. Take free walking tours
19. Bring emergency cash
20. Bring good shoes
21. Get the travel insurance
22. Don't overplan your trip
23. Be respectful to the locals of your destination
24. Bring an extra phone charger and adapter
25. Enjoy!
